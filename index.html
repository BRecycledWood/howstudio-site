Methodology

The five
HOWs

Before a line of code. Before a brand color. Five questions we ask. Every single time.

Fig. 1 — Build methodology

How does this help real people?

If we can't answer this in one sentence, we're not ready to build. Every decision traces back to real human benefit.

01

How do we ship it faster?

Speed isn't reckless — it's ruthless prioritization. Cut scope, keep impact. Launch in weeks, not months.

02

How do we make it better than last time?

Every project teaches us something. We document what worked, fix what didn't, and compound our knowledge.

03

How do we build it to scale?

Not "enterprise-ready" — just smart architecture that doesn't break when you grow. Simple systems that handle complexity.

04

How do we know it's working?

Metrics that matter. Not vanity analytics — actual proof people are getting value. Measure impact, not activity.
